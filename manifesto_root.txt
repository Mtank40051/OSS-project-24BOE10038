  MY OPEN SOURCE MANIFESTO
  Author : root
  Date   : 28 March 2026
================================================================

I am a part of a world built on openness. Every day, I rely on
python — a tool that someone chose to build in the open and share
freely, not for profit, but because they believed that knowledge
belongs to everyone. That act of generosity shapes everything I do.

To me, freedom means independence. In the context of software, that
word is not abstract — it is the right to read the code that runs
my computer, to change it when it does not serve me, and to share
those changes with others who might benefit. Without that freedom,
technology becomes a cage instead of a tool.

I commit to contributing to this tradition. One day, I will build
ai and release it openly — not to gain credit, but because
the open-source community gave me everything I know, and the only
honest response to that gift is to pass it forward.

The software I write will be free as in freedom.

  — root, 28 March 2026
